About GrowFi Learning LLC

GrowFi Learning LLC is an educational technology company focused on making financial literacy accessible to the next generation. We believe that understanding investing and personal finance should start early, in a safe and engaging environment.

Our flagship product, the GrowFi app, provides a risk-free stock market simulator paired with a structured curriculum and AI-powered coaching to help teens build real investing knowledge and confidence.

GrowFi was designed and developed by a teen entrepreneur who saw the need for an approachable, hands-on way to learn about the stock market.
